FIRST Robotics Competition Team 1277 Team Website

FRC is a competition between High Schools across the world to build a industrial sized robot to compete in a competition. Teams are given 6 weeks to design, build, and program a robot to accomplish a certain challenge given certain rules and specifications. These challenges involve using the robot to manipulate objects in the playing field to score points, and the year that I competed in involved climbing onto an elevated platform to score extra points.
